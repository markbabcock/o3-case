Services

Our Services

Field Nurse
Case Management

We provide traditional field nurse case management working with and directly meeting with the client/injured worker to facilitate appropriate medical care that may include physical therapy, referrals to specialists, surgeries and all coordination required to help bring the injured worker back to health and pre-injury activities. We handle each case with personalized and detailed attention implementing the approved and appropriate care as recommended by treating physicians. We address all challenges of the client in a proactive and open-minded manner.

Catastrophic Nurse
Case Management

We provide catastrophic nurse case management for clients after significant, complex, life-changing injuries. We assess, plan, implement and evaluate the urgent and long-term medical needs of the client. This care necessitates plans and goals for the clients evolving medical and emotional support. We communicate with all parties transparently as the client's medical care needs evolve in this complex setting to promote continuity of care for the client.

Task
Assignments

We accept short term nurse case management assignments. Task assignments could include scheduling or attending appointments with clients once or intermittently to assess medical care needs and or address concerns of the client/ physician.

Telephonic
Case Management

Telephonic nurse case management can be provided to coordinate patient/client medical care via telephone. Nurse case managers can assess the clients medical care needs and collaborate directly with client and their treating physicians to coordinate appropriate medical care services and care plans.
